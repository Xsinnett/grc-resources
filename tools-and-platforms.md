# GRC Tools & Platforms

A curated list of tools and platforms used by compliance and GRC 
professionals. Organized by category to help you find the right 
tool for the job.

---

## 1. GRC Platforms

### OneTrust
**Website:** https://www.onetrust.com  
**Best for:** Privacy management, consent management, and GRC  
**Overview:** One of the most widely used privacy and GRC platforms 
on the market. Covers privacy program management, cookie consent, 
data mapping, vendor risk management, and more.  
**Pricing:** Enterprise — contact for pricing

### ServiceNow GRC
**Website:** https://www.servicenow.com/products/governance-risk-compliance.html  
**Best for:** Large enterprises managing integrated risk  
**Overview:** A comprehensive GRC platform built on the ServiceNow 
ecosystem. Strong for policy management, audit management, and 
risk management in large organizations.  
**Pricing:** Enterprise — contact for pricing

### LogicGate Risk Cloud
**Website:** https://www.logicgate.com  
**Best for:** Mid-size organizations looking for flexible GRC workflows  
**Overview:** A no-code GRC platform that allows teams to build 
custom risk and compliance workflows without engineering support.  
**Pricing:** Contact for pricing

### Drata
**Website:** https://drata.com  
**Best for:** Automating SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other compliance frameworks  
**Overview:** Drata automates compliance monitoring and evidence 
collection, making it popular with SaaS companies pursuing SOC 2 
or ISO 27001 certification.  
**Pricing:** Contact for pricing

### Vanta
**Website:** https://www.vanta.com  
**Best for:** Startups and growing companies pursuing SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA  
**Overview:** Similar to Drata, Vanta automates security monitoring 
and compliance workflows. Very popular in the startup ecosystem.  
**Pricing:** Starts around $7,500/year

## 2. Privacy Management Tools

### TrustArc
**Website:** https://trustarc.com  
**Best for:** Privacy program management and cookie consent  
**Overview:** A comprehensive privacy management platform covering 
assessments, consent management, data mapping, and privacy 
request management.  
**Pricing:** Contact for pricing

### Osano
**Website:** https://www.osano.com  
**Best for:** Small to mid-size businesses managing cookie consent 
and privacy compliance  
**Overview:** A user-friendly privacy platform with strong cookie 
consent management and vendor monitoring features. More accessible 
pricing than enterprise alternatives.  
**Pricing:** Starts at $199/month

### Termly
**Website:** https://termly.io  
**Best for:** Small businesses needing privacy policies and 
cookie consent banners  
**Overview:** A simple and affordable tool for generating privacy 
policies, terms of service, and cookie consent banners. Great 
starting point for small businesses.  
**Pricing:** Free plan available — paid plans start at $10/month

## 6. Free & Open Source Tools

### OpenSCAP
**Website:** https://www.open-scap.org  
**Best for:** Automated compliance checking  
**Overview:** An open source framework for maintaining enterprise 
security and compliance. Supports NIST, PCI DSS, and other 
standards.  
**Pricing:** Free

### CISA Resources
**Website:** https://www.cisa.gov/resources-tools  
**Best for:** Free cybersecurity tools and resources from the 
U.S. government  
**Overview:** The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency 
provides a library of free tools and resources for organizations 
of all sizes.  
**Pricing:** Free

### NIST Tools & Resources
**Website:** https://www.nist.gov/cybersecurity  
**Best for:** Frameworks, guidelines, and tools from NIST  
**Overview:** NIST provides free access to all of its cybersecurity 
frameworks, special publications, and guidance documents.  
**Pricing:** Free
